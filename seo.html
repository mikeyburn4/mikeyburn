What is SEO?

Having trouble getting your site noticed? It might be time to delve into Search Engine Optimization, commonly known as SEO. While this subject can seem complex, at its core, SEO requires thorough research. Optimizing your site involves various elements such as naturally integrating key words, alternative text for images, ensuring mobile friendliness, and even avoiding specific key words. These actions are just small pieces of what search engines consider when assessing a site's relevance and authority. And I'd be delighted to assist you with this procedure.
